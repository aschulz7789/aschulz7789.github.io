What is Tommy's Bakery?

Tommy's Bakery is an all natural gluten-free bakery originating in Los Angeles, California. Family owned, Tommy's bakery believes every customer is important, and every meal should be treated as a special event.

Here at Tommy's we have a different specialty for each meal of the day, all of which are displayed to the right.

Breakfast is served from open until 11:00 AM, specialties are bagels and muffins.

Lunch is served from 11:00 AM until 4:00 PM, and our specialty is the world-renowned Tommy's french bread pizza.

Starting at 4:00 PM Tommy's turns into a dessert attraction, with gluten-free cookies and cupcakes.
