Tommy's Gluten-Free Bakery



What is Tommy's Bakery?

Tommy's Bakery is an all natural gluten-free bakery originating in Los Angeles, California. Family owned, Tommy's bakery believes every customer is important, and every meal should be treated as a special event.

Hours of Operation

  • Sunday - 10 AM through 7 PM
  • Monday to Friday - 8 AM to 9 PM
  • Saturday - 9 AM through 11 PM
